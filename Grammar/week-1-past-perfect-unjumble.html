Quick reference: past perfect

Form: had + past participle — e.g. had finished, hadn't seen, Had you traveled...?

Use: to show that one past action happened before another past action.

  • Common signal words: before, after, already, by the time, when, never...until
  • The earlier action = past perfect. The later action = simple past.
